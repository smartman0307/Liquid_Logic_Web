Liquidlogic - Social Care Software & IT System Suppliers

Liquidlogic is the UK’s fastest-growing supplier of software for local authority social care and education management services. Liquidlogic’s social care software is built on a modern platform that is flexible, intuitive, secure, and constantly refreshed. Developed specifically by and for social workers, the social care IT systems make case management and record-keeping simple. Our range of social work software is the leading solution for councils for both adults and children's services.

Liquidlogic’s parent company is System C, a leading supplier of healthcare IT to the NHS. System C's products include the CareFlow Electronic Patient Record, as well as the CarePlus child health solution, and more recently the National Immunisation Management Service (NIMS) - IT software that supports the management of the influenza and Covid-19 vaccination programmes across England.
